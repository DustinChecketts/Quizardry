About Quizardry

This app was created by 3 kids. Our family loves Disney. Our dad wanted us to learn to code. He helped us pick an easy game to code and showed us ChatGPT. He helped us when we got stuck.

The only theme right now is Disney but we want to add more for some movies and video games.

We hope you like it!
